How to do an Oil Change

Step 5

Remove the oil plug and let oil drain

Step 6

Remove the oil filter and lube the new filter with oil and pour a bit inside

Step 7

Screw in the new filter

Step 8

Pour the rest of the oil into the engine

Step 9

Turn on the car to get the pressure up

Step 10

Done!
