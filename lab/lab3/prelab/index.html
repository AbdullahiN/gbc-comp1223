Campus Life

A great college experience is about finding balance. Of course you want to study hard, excel in your courses and gain work-ready skills. But you also want to make friends, experience new things and have fun. At George Brown College, there are a variety of ways you can get involved while preparing for your chosen career. We offer everything from student activities and politics, to athletics, to a range of clubs so you have the opportunity to shape your own unique college experience.

Defining Math Anxiety
Math anxiety is comparable to the stage fright many people experience when speaking in front of a large audience. As defined by psychologists, math anxiety occurs when students experience helplessness, mental disorganization and physical stress (such as shortness of breath) while doing math.
